The Global Hunger Crisis

The Reality We Face:

  • 828 million people go to bed hungry every night

  • 45 million children under 5 are wasted
  • 149 million children are stunted
  • Every 10 seconds, a child dies from hunger
🚨

Despite producing enough food to feed 10 billion people, hunger persists due to poverty, conflict, climate change, and inefficient distribution systems.
